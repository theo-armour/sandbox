## Excerpt

Applied epistemology refers to the study that determines whether the systems of investigation that seek the truth lead to true beliefs about the world.&#91;1&#93; A specific conceptualization cites that it attempts to reveal whether these systems contribute to epistemic aims.&#91;2&#93; It is applied in practices outside of philosophy like science and mathematics.&#91;1&#93;
