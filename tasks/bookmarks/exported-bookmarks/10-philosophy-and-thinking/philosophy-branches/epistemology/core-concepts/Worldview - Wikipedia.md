## Excerpt

A worldview (also world-view) or Weltanschauung is the fundamental cognitive orientation of an individual or society encompassing the whole of the individual's or society's knowledge, culture, and point of view.&#91;1&#93;  A worldview can include natural philosophy; fundamental, existential, and normative postulates; or themes, values, emotions, and ethics.&#91;2&#93;
