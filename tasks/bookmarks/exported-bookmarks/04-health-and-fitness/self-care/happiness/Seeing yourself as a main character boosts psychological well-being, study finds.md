# Seeing yourself as a main character boosts psychological well-being, study finds

**URL:** https://www.psypost.org/seeing-yourself-as-a-main-character-boosts-psychological-well-being-study-finds/

**Created:** 2024-07-20T22:20:13.110Z

**Tags:** 

**Favorite:** false

## Excerpt

People who see themselves as major characters in their life stories experience higher well-being and greater satisfaction of basic psychological needs compared to those who view themselves as minor characters.
