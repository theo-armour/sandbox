## Excerpt

Every important thought in Scott Belsky’s head immediately goes into his todo list. And every item on Scott Belsky’s todo list starts with a verb: Discuss Follow up Meet Ensure Track That’s because the important things to Scott Belsky are the things he can take action on. Everything else is just noise.
