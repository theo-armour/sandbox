Knowledge representation and reasoning (KRR, KR&R, KRÂ²) is the field of artificial intelligence (AI) dedicated to representing information about the world in a form that a computer system can use to solve complex tasks such as diagnosing a medical condition or having a dialog in a natural language. Knowledge representation incorporates findings from psychology&#91;1&#93; about how humans solve problems and represent knowledge, in order to design formalisms that will make complex systems easier to design and build.
