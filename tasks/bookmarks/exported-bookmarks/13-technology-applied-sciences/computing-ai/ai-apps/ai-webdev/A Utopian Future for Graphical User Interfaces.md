## Excerpt

Can we imagine a better future for creating graphical user interfaces? Let’s scope “creating” to: visual design, writing code, managing a team, or authoring content & text. For each of these disciplines, and especially the collaborative interfaces between them, certainly the future will be better than today. But how so? And why? How do we get there?
