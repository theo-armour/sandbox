## Excerpt

Announced earlier this year, the Asus NUC 14 Pro and NUC 14 Pro+ are now available for purchase. The former starts at $394.99 and comes with up to Intel Core Ultra 7 155H, while the latter is available with Core Ultra 9 185H at $869.
